How to compete

  1. Install the myinsights skill.
  2. Say /myinsights submit. It computes your scorecard locally, shows you the exact 25-number payload, and asks once. On yes it forks, commits, and opens the PR for you. Merge = on the board.
  3. Resubmit any time with the same command. Say delete me from the leaderboard to remove yourself — no questions.

The one manual step is deliberate: nothing is ever submitted without you seeing the payload and saying yes. Prefer to do it by hand? PR scores/<handle>/<YYYY-MM-DD>.json yourself — CI validates the allowlist either way.

This is a voluntary community game, not a performance instrument. Participation is opt-in, handles are pseudonymous, submissions are a strict allowlist (never transcripts, projects, hours, or code), deletion is self-service, and this board must never be used in performance reviews, ranking discussions, or management reporting. If that framing breaks, the board shuts down.
